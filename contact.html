Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn EcoPoints?

Simply show your QR code at participating stores after making eco-friendly purchases. Points are automatically added to your account!

Which stores accept EcoPoints?

We partner with major retailers like Reliance Fresh, DMart, Big Bazaar, and many more. Check our app for the complete list.

How can I redeem my points?

Points can be redeemed for discounts, eco-friendly products, or donated to environmental causes through our platform.

Is EcoPoints free to use?

Yes! EcoPoints is completely free for consumers. We're funded by our retail partners who want to promote sustainable shopping.
